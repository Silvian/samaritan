Samaritan CMA is designed to address the long standing needs for churches and other organisations to better manage their internal administrative affairs:

  • The ability to access and view the entire up to date members list securely and fully accessible on the cloud anywhere in the world and on any device.

  • A simple and easy way to to add members, guests, assign members to groups/organisations, assign members to roles, and keep track of members who have left.

  • A very intuitive user interface which is mobile friendly and easily understood.
